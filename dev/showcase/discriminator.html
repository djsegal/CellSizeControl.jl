The slope is the strategy

The cleanest fingerprint of a size-control strategy is the slope of division size on birth size: ~0 is a sizer (divides at a fixed size, ignoring birth size), ~1 an adder, ~2 a timer. Slide the control strength and watch the cloud rotate through all three:

(0 / 1 / 2 are the Soifer–Amir 2016 reference slopes the package's L1 tests assert.)
